Brute-Force Problems

These are by far the most common problems in the USACO Bronze category. It is almost guaranteed that there will be at least one brute force contest in a USACO Bronze contest, and sometimes there will be 2 (and rarely 3).

A brute-force problem is what it sounds like: simply doing everything possible to eventually reatch the answer. Let's say you were trying to achieve the password to someone's phone (assume 4 digits from 0 to 9). A brute force solution would be to try every single password possible. This way, it is guaranteed that at one point, the answer would be correct.

One of the biggest issues to a brute force solution is time.
